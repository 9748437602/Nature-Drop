Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the 'My Orders' section. We'll also send you email updates with tracking information once your order ships. If you're having trouble tracking your order, please contact our support team.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Items must be unused, in their original packaging, and with all tags attached. To initiate a return, please visit the 'Returns' section in your account or contact our customer service team.

Some items are non-returnable for hygiene reasons, including earphones and other personal electronics. Please check the product page for specific return restrictions.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days. We also offer expedited shipping options at checkout:

  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days
  • Priority Shipping: 2-3 business days
  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days

Delivery times may vary during holidays or sale periods.

How can I change or cancel my order?

You can change or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it. After this time, your order will be processed for shipping and cannot be modified. To request a change or cancellation:

  1. Go to 'My Orders' in your account
  2. Select the order you wish to change
  3. Click the 'Modify Order' button

If the option is unavailable, your order has already entered the shipping process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods for your convenience:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • PayPal
  • UPI Payments
  • Net Banking
  • Wallet Payments (Paytm, PhonePe, etc.)

All payments are securely processed through our encrypted payment gateway.

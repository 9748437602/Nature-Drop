About Us

Where Technology Meets Sustainability

At Nature Drop, we believe in harmonizing technological advancement with environmental responsibility. Founded in 2018, we've grown from a small startup to a trusted name in eco-friendly electronics, serving over 500,000 satisfied customers across India.

Our Mission

Nature Drop was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to make sustainable electronics accessible to everyone. In a world increasingly concerned about environmental impact, we saw an opportunity to transform the electronics industry through eco-conscious innovation.

Our journey began when our founder, Priya Sharma, noticed the alarming amount of electronic waste generated during her time in the tech industry. She envisioned a company that would not only provide cutting-edge technology but also prioritize the planet at every step of the process.

Sustainable Innovation

We constantly explore new ways to reduce environmental impact without compromising on technology quality.

Customer Empowerment

We provide tools and knowledge to help our customers make eco-friendly choices with confidence.

Community Impact

We believe in creating positive change not just for individuals, but for communities and ecosystems.

From our humble beginnings in a small Mumbai office with just three employees, we've grown into a team of 85 passionate individuals across four major Indian cities. Our growth has been fueled by our commitment to environmental responsibility and technological excellence.

In 2020, we launched our flagship recycling program, which has successfully diverted over 15 tons of electronic waste from landfills. This initiative not only helps the environment but also provides refurbished devices to underprivileged students across India.

Our Environmental Commitment

At Nature Drop, sustainability isn't just a buzzword - it's the core of everything we do. We've implemented comprehensive environmental practices throughout our operations:

  • Carbon-Neutral Shipping: All deliveries are offset through reforestation projects
  • Plastic-Free Packaging: We use 100% biodegradable and compostable materials
  • Energy-Efficient Operations: Our facilities run on 80% solar power
  • Product Lifecycle Program: We take back devices for proper recycling

We're proud to have reduced our carbon footprint by 45% since 2020 and are committed to becoming a carbon-negative company by 2025.
